TITLE: Chateau aerien. Ballon captif a cloisons etanches, systeme Tobiansky. Exposition universelle 1894.

SUMMARY: Belgian poster shows an air station suspended by a captive balloon at the 1894 world's fair in Antwerp, Belgium. Advertises Alfred Tobiansky d'Altoff's patented device for moving passengers from a platform on the ground to the air station.

MEDIUM: 1 photomechanical print (poster) : photolithograph, color.

CREATED/PUBLISHED: Brussels : Photo-litho arts graphiques, 1894.
NOTES: Partial parallel titles in English and German. Tissandier collection.
