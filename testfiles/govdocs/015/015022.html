The U.S. Consulate in Montreal - Ambassador Wilkins' visit to the Château Ramezay Museum

Ambassador Wilkins' visit to the Château Ramezay Museum


Ambassador David H. Wilkins makes remarks in the Salle de Nantes room at the Château Ramezay Museum.


From left to right: André Delisle, General Director and Curator at the Château Ramezay Museum, Claude Boutet, President of the Museum's Board of Directors, David H. Wilkins, U.S. Ambassador to Canada, and Mary B. Marshall, U.S. Consul General in Montreal.


David H. Wilkins signs the guest book with, in the background, the portrait of Benjamin Franklin who visited the Château Ramezay in 1776.
