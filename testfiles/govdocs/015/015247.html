General Comment

Please understand that elephants are large animals who need big spaces. Many
zoos and circuses limit the space provided to these magnificent animals causing
disease and other disorders as well as documented neurotic behaviors.
Domination and agressive use of bull hooks must be outlawed. Elephants must be
allowed room to roam rather than be confined for extended periods. I support the
elephant sanctuary movement in this country and ask that you help to improve
conditions for captive elephants in this country. Thank you very much.
