Summary
(English)
Law 21979 of 4/17/79 updates the maximum and minimum amounts of fines established by article 1 of Decree Law 4686 of 1958, ratified by Law 14467, for violations of the provisions regarding the import and export trade, regulated by Decree 6603 of 1962. The updating of these fines shall take place twice a year. (3 pv.)
