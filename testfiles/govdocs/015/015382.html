    3. Project Focus:

    • Project Primary Focus: Ecological Effects
    • Project Secondary Focus:

    4. Description:

      Within the St.Lawrence Action Plan, this network, in using indicator species, contributes to evaluate the condition of the St.Lawrence , based on changes in the composition, structure and functions of its ecosystem and to determine if toxic substances are sufficiently high to cause health effects in wildlife. Non destructive biomarkers have been selected as indicators of exposure to and effects of contaminants. They are linked to measures of contaminants and reproductive success to give a picture of the condition of this system.
