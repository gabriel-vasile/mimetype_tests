This provision defines terms related to discrimination, including unlawful discrimination, atypical hereditary cellular blood trait, sickle cell trait, hemoglobin C trait, thalassemia trait, tay-sachs trait, cystic fibrosis trait, genetic characteristic, genetic information, and genetic test.
