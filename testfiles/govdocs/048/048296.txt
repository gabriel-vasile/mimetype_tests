_Contents_

Braille Scores Catalog--Organ is a complete list of organ music
in the National Library Service for the Blind and Physically
Handicapped Music Section collection as of June 1994.

 This catalog is divided into three general sections:  a
listing of collections containing music by more than one
composer, a listing of titles arranged alphabetically by
composer, and a short list of organ methods and teaching
materials.

 By using the index, readers can locate all available braille
music by a given composer, including music in collections. 
Distinctive and descriptive titles are also indexed, but
repetitious titles such as forms or tempo indication terms are
not.  For example, March upon Handel's "Lift up your heads" and
Variations on "America" are both indexed, but the words March
and Variations alone will not be found.  Similarly, Adagio,
Allegro, and Largo by themselves are not listed in the index.

 Thematic catalog numbers are provided for many works by the
composers listed below.  Print thematic catalogs can be found in
large music collections of public and college libraries.
