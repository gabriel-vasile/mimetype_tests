Flood Impacts
37.0 Major flooding continues. Southern Railroad Bridge flooded out.
33.0 Major flooding expands. Highway 341 bridge is flooded.
30.0 Major flooding continues. Entire town of Lumber City will be cut off and isolated by Ocmulgee River...and swollen local creeks and small streams will occur. Some businesses and homes will flood near river...and water reaches the under supports of the Highway 341 bridge.
23.0 Major flooding begins. Mobile home floods on southside of Highway 341 bridge.
18.0 Moderate flooding begins. Flooding of access road to boat ramp near Highway 341 bridge occurs.
15.0 Flood Stage is reached. Minor flooding begins. Water overflows into the broad swampy flood plain. Farm fields on right bank flood. General agriculture and timber interest affected by flooding. In addition...water reaches top of boat ramp near Highway 341 Bridge.
