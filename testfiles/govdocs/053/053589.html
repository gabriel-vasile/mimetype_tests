Overview

The Advanced Technology Program (ATP) provides cutting edge expertise and technologies primarily in support of NCI investigators, although other Government and non-Government scientists can also access most of the research support services offered through the Economy Act and Work For Others programs respectively.

 

ATP Director Dr. Tim Harris joined SAIC-Frederick, Inc., in January 2007. Dr. Harris has many years of experience in RNA research, including several years on mRNA and Picornaviruses. He also brings a wealth of industry as well as research experience to the program, as former President and CEO of Novasite Pharmaceuticals and Founder CEO of Structural GenomiX. He was also the Vice President of R&D at Sequana and earlier in his career was Director of Biotechnology for Glaxo Research Group and Group Leader at Celltech Ltd. Dr. Harris is very open to any questions or other inquiries about the Program and can be reached at harristjr@mail.nih.gov or 301-846-1144.

The other members of the ATP management team (in addition to the Program's individual laboratory directors) are Jeffrey Lake (operations) and Bruce Crise (business development). Both Jeffrey and Bruce are available to answer any questions you might have about the program and can be reached at lakejeff@mail.nih.gov, 301-846-5758 and criseb@ncifcrf.gov, 301-846-5739, respectively.
