Dear Colleagues

I am a project manager for the Embedding Literacy, Language and Numeracy
(LLN) in London Local Authorities Project, which is working with 11
authorities to embed LLN development in their systems and processes,
including in recruitment, induction and performance appraisal.

We are currently looking at the subject of specifying LLN competencies
within job descriptions / profiles. We are aware of some work that has
been done on this in the UK through the Sector Skills Councils and one
small Government-funded project, but I was wondering if other colleagues
have experience of this process and might be able to pass on their
insights, or are perhaps aware of other projects, reports etc that could
be of interest?

Any information would be greatly appreciated!
