Services Friday for Lab employee Eaton

A funeral mass for Lawrie Eaton is at 11 a.m. Friday in Immaculate Heart of Mary Catholic Church in Los Alamos. Eaton, 54, died Tuesday at his Los Alamos home.

Eaton, a former group leader of Tritium Science and Engineering (ESA-TSE), joined the Laboratory in 1986 in the former Accelerator Technology Division. He later joined the Engineering Sciences and Applications (ESA) Division.

Eaton is survived by his wife Cindy of Network Engineering (CCN-5) and three daughters.
