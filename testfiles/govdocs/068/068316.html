Here at Cascades Job Corps Center we can pave the road for your achievements, listen to your dreams for your future, and perhaps offer some new ideas for a vocation or a trade that you hadn't considered. We are here to help YOU. This Department of Labor program has been effective for more than 25 years. There are many success stories of people able to achieve much more than they ever planned on when starting out.

How to Find Us

Here is how you begin the enrollment process:
Call the National Toll-Free Number 800-733-5627 for a Link to Your Future.
They will Refer you to the Nearest Admissions Counselor.

Admissions Counselors Serving Cascades Job Corps Center and other Job Corps Centers in the Western Region are Available to Talk to You at the Locations Listed Below.

Job Corps has Admissions Counselors in many towns and cities throughout the country. A short talk with one of them can help you decide if Job Corps can help you realize your dreams.

Feel free to use e-mail to contact the admissions counselor to whom the National Toll-Free representative referred you. Your admissions counselor can give you the correct e-mail address when you call, or, here is a listing of some of the frequently-used admissions counselors serving Cascades Job Corps Center (May 3, 2007). When you send a message, be sure to add your name, mailing address, phone number, best time to call, nature of request, and e-mail address.
