Abstract:
CLIPQD24 Coverage The Clipqd24 coverage includes the 29 1:24000 USGS 7.5 minute quadrangles that cover the C&O Canal NHP, National Park Service. The coverage is one polygon.

The quadrangle list includes:

1. Cumberland MD-PA-WVA 2. Cresaptown, WVA-MD 3. Evitts Creek MD-PA-WVA 4. Patterson Creek MD-WVA 5. Oldtown MD-WVA 6. Paw Paw WVA-MD 7. Artemas MD-PA-WVA 8. Bellegrove MD-PA-WVA 9. Great Cacapon WVA-MD 10. Hancock WVA-MD-PA 11. Cherry Run MD-WVA-PA 12. Big Pool MD-WVA 13. Hedgesville WVA-MD 14. Williamsport MD-WVA 15. Shepherdstown WVA-MD 16. Keedysville MD-VA 17. Harpers Ferry VA-MD-WVA 18. Charles Town WVA-VA-MD 19. Point of Rocks MD-VA 20. Buckeystown MD-VA 21. Poolesville MD-VA 22. Waterford VA-MD 23. Leesburg VA-MD 24. Sterling VA-MD 25. Seneca MD-VA 26. Rockville MD-VA 27. Vienna VA-MMD 28. Falls Church VA-MD 29. Washington West DC-MD-VA

Purpose:
The coverage may be used specifically for creating a project area for some of the Potomac River and the C&O Canal NHP.

Entity_and_Attribute_Overview:
Clipqd24.pat has Area, Perimeter Clipqd24# and clipqd24-ID with standard Arc/Info attributes.

Process: Combined all 29 quads by using a quad sheet coverage. The quads applicable to the park were singled out. The coverage was projected into Decimal Degrees. The basis for the coverage (quads sheet) was derived from a 7.5 minute quad map which then was parallel copied multiple times. The result is an outline coverage of all 29 quads with a boundry outline.

The other steps are unknown as the editor Tina Kapka, is no longer with the NPS.

Revisions: This is the only version which exists.
