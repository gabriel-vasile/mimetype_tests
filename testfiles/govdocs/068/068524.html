Gen5 Minintuple Status for LBL MTM Analysis

This page documents the current status of the Gen5 minintuples used in the LBL MTM top mass analysis. Bold samples indicate our main samples -- the ones we usually run CAF attacks on.

WARNING!

Most new minintuples are "loose" minintuples (require 4 jets of at least 10 GeV) and pretag (no b-tag required). Thus, you should take care to apply the appropriate selection requirements when using these minintuples for further studies.

Minintuples are located in /auto/pdsfdv48/cdf/plujan/minintuples.

All sample information taken from the Gen5 Top MC page.

All skimmed samples are taken from the loose skims in /auto/pdsfdv70a/cdf/plujan/ntuples_533nt_skim.

All minintuples are built with top_533_nt5. All data minintuples are built using ROOT 4 (v612) but still using the Gen5 code.

The various notations in the "selected events" indicate the criteria used to select the sample in question:

  • (6) means jetCorr06b was used, (4) means jetCorr04 was used (only for some very old and now obsolete minintuples)
  • (L) means loose selection (4 jets of at least 10 GeV at L4), (T) means tight selection (4 jets of at least 15 GeV at L4)
  • (P) means pretag, otherwise at least 1 b-tag is required
