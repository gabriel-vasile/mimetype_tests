    CLASS D11,JEWELRY, SYMBOLIC INSIGNIA, AND ORNAMENTS
Click here for a printable version of this file

SECTION I - CLASS DEFINITION

This class provides for design patents claiming ornamental designs for:

1. Fastener, Buckle or Restraint for Apparel or Personal Belongings Not Elsewhere Specified

2. Jewelry

3. Symbolic Medal, Badge or Insignia

4. Artificial Plant Life and Holiday Season Decorations

5. Sculpture, Table or Wall Ornament

6. Flag or Pennant

7. Miscellaneous

(1) Note. Design patents in this class are classified by what is claimed and shown in full lines only. Broken, hatched or stippled lines, which may be included in design patent drawings, are considered as environment only.
