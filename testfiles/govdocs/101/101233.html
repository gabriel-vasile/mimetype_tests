This document presents analysis and supporting data for the provisions EPA used for establishing the proposed nonconformance penalties for model year 2004 and later on-highway heavy duty diesel engines. The penalty rate is determined by a penalty curve with a slope for engines with compliance levels near the standard equal to the 90th percentile marginal cost of compliance.
