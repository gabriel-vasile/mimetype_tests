Procurement

  Purchase Requisition Review for Quality-related Requirements - DL-QAP-0507

  Evaluation of Seller Quality Assurance (QA) Programs - (SBMS Subject Area)

  • A seller's quality assurance program needs to be evaluated for the procurement of items with an ESH&Q risk level of High (A1-Critical) or Moderate (A2-Major).  An ISO 9000 database is available through the NSLS Quality Manager (QM) that can help simplify this process if the seller is ISO certified.  Contact the NSLS QM for assistance in this area.
