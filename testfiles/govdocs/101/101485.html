GTG Map Gallery

Have you created a map on an incident utilizing the Standards and want to share it? Send your map in to us, and we will post the best ones on this site!

Guidelines

  • Your map should utilize the standards in the SOP. A map does not need to utilize all of the standards, but should demonstrate the practical application of the standards.
  • We are especially interested in seeing unique maps using new technologies and styles. For example, send us graphics of ways that you have used Google Earth to support an incident!
  • Please submit either a jpeg or pdf of your map.
  • Submit your map to Skip Edel at sedel@lamar.colostate.edu. Either send your map as an attachment to an email or place it on an ftp site and send Skip Edel an email with the address of the ftp site.
