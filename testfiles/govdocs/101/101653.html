DTP - Natural Product Extract Cancer Screening Data

Cell Line Screening Data for Families in the Phylum Crustacean
These files contain results from the Human Tumor Cell Line Screen for extracts from organisms classified in the listed familes. These are comma delimited text files with 14 fields. These data are provided by the Natural Products Branch of the Developmental Therapeutics Progam, Division of Cancer Treatment, Diagnosis and Centers, National Cancer Institute.

  • Paguridae
  • Pandalidae
  • Scyllaridae
