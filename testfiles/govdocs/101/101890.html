Request for Quotations number SMT850-08-S-001

Enclosed is a Request for Quotations for, Hardware,software and other IT related items as listed below or other similar  brands with the same specifications.

If you would like to submit a quotation, follow the specifications in this solicitation document and  e-mail quote or send by post to the attention of the General Services Office, Development House – 3rd Floor, St. Anne St. Floriana VLT 01, Malta .
Your quotation MUST be in a sealed envelope marked with the Solicitation Number SMT850-08-S-001:
