Topic:          Data for ecosystems analysis

Speaker:        Lee De Cola, NMD


Lee argues that all data exist in a conceptual space of four
dimensions. He demonstrates -- with examples -- how a simple set-
theoretic ordering suggests virtually all questions that might arise in
the modeling and management of an ecosystem.
