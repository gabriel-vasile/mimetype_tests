IA #73-03 - 11/1/88, "Automatic Detention of Energy Point
            Stimulators" ATTACH 2/14/96



TYPE OF ALERT: Automatic Detention

PRODUCT   :    Energy Point Stimulators (Medical Devices)

PRODUCT CODE :  89[][][][][]

PROBLEM   :    No Premarket approval application has been
               submitted

COUNTRY   :    Known Countries:  England (925) and Netherlands
               Antilles including Curacao (640)

MANUFACTURER/
SHIPPER   :    Various (See attachment)

CHARGE    :    "The article is subject to refusal of admission
               pursuant to Section 801(a)(3) in that it appears
               to be adulterated under Section 501 (f)(1)(B) in
               that an application for premarket approval has not
               been submitted."

RECOMMENDING
OFFICE    :    Center for Devices & Radiological Health, Office
               of Compliance (HFZ-300).

REASON FOR
ALERT     :    To prevent continuing importation of a medical
               device that does not have premarket approval.

          Since 1985 the agency has been aware of the use and
          distribution of these devices.  Books and literature
          distributed with them claim that they are useful for
          non-invasive acupuncture.  The devices are intended for
          trigger-point location of low-impedence or "energy
          points" (acupuncture points) on the skin.

INSTRUCTIONS   :    Automatically detain all Energy Point Stimulators.  Firms
          listed in the attachment to this alert have previously shipped these
          devices to importers/distributors in the United States.

FOI  :    No purging required.
