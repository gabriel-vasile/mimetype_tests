SYNERGISTIC ACTIVITIES
---------------------------------

Chair of DOE ARM Aerosol Working Group and member of ARM Steering and Executive Committee (2005 – present)

Lead scientist for DOE ARM September 2005 Aerosol Lidar Validation Experiment (ALIVE).

Platform scientist for portions of the EVE ( Monterey , 2004) and INTEX-NA/ITCT ( Gulf of Maine , 2004) missions. Direction and execution of research flights of the CIRPAS Twin Otter and Sky Research J-31 aircraft.

Led planning of DOE ARM May 2003 Aerosol Intensive Observation Period. Defined payload on CIRPAS Twin Otter aircraft. Led 10 principal investigators during field campaign as platform scientist.

Employed NASA Ames Airborne Sun photometers in campaigns all over the world and made processed data available to the research community through web-based archives.

Extensive experience in aerosol closure studies, lidar and satellite validation.

Associate Editor, Journal Geophysical Research – Clouds and Aerosols (since 2002).

NASA Group Achievement Awards: INTEX (2004) and SOLVE II (2002) Science Teams.
