Contaminant Information

Temephos

CAS Number: 3383-96-8

Basic Information

Synonym(s): O,O,O',O'-tetramethyl O,O'-(thiodi-4,1-phenylene) phosphorothioate; O,O,O',O'-tetramethyl O,O'-thiodi-p-phenylene phosphorothioate; O,O'-(thiodi-4,1-phenylene)bis(O,O'-dimethylphosphorothioate)
Formula: C16H20O6P2S3
 Molecular Weight: 466.46
 Melting Point (°C): 30 - 30.5
Contaminant Type: Temephos is an insecticide.
Notes: Solid

Solubility (in water)

  • Solubility: 0.03 mg/l ak
    Temperature (°C): 25
  • Solubility: 0.001 mg/l h

Half-Life (in soil unless otherwise noted)

  • t1/2: ~30 days h
