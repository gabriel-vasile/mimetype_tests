U.S. Global Trade and Technology Network (GTN)

GTN is a United States Agency for International Development (USAID) program which facilitates sustainable economic growth in developing countries and emerging markets through business linkages and technology transfer. A key resource is the internet-based trade facilitation platform allowing qualified U.S. companies to find international partners from a database of 12,000 companies.
