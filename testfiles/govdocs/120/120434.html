Whole Wildlife Toxicology Catalog

In March of 2007, nearly 50 scientists and administrators from the U.S. and Canada participated in a Smithsonian-sponsored Wildlife Toxicology Workshop. Invitees were from academic, government, conservation and the private organizations and were selected to represent the diverse disciplines that encompass wildlife toxicology. One of the many action items derived from the workshop was the creation of a web portal for wildlife toxicology-oriented web sites and databases. The Whole Wildlife Toxicology Catalog attempts to compile sites containing information that will be of value to scientists, regulators and natural resource managers.


This web portal is served by the USGS Patuxent Wildlife Research Center.   Contact brattner@usgs.gov to submit candidate sites to the catalog.
The USGS does not maintain or endorse the content of some of the linked sites.
