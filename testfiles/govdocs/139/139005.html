About us

The NASA Robotics Academy is an elite internship at NASA that selects people with prior experience in robotics. The students chosen come from all over the world and are typically undergraduate students. Once the program has started, they are divided into teams of four and are assigned to real NASA projects. Apart from their team projects, the NASA Robotics Academy interns are required to work together on a group project. This year, the interns decided to use Mousey the Mouse Bot to inspire younger kids in elementary school through the High School level to get involved in robotics.
