March 24, 1998

AMY BRADLEY

DESCRIPTION

Date of Birth: May 12, 1974 Place of Birth: Petersburg, Virginia
Sex: Female Hair: Short Brown
Height: 5'6" Eyes: Green
Weight: 120 pounds Race: White

THE DETAILS

Amy Bradley was reported missing in the early morning hours of March 24, 1998. She was last seen by family members sitting on the balcony outside their cabin aboard the Rhapsody of the Seas cruise ship. The ship was en route to Curacao, Antilles at the time Amy was last seen. The ship docked in Curacao shortly after Amy was discovered missing. Extensive searches on the ship and at sea have produced no signs of Amy's whereabouts. This investigation is ongoing.

REMARKS

Amy Bradley graduated from a college in Virginia with a degree in physical education. She enjoys playing basketball. She would have begun a job working with a computer company upon her return from the trip.

Amy Bradley has the following tattoos: a Tasmanian Devil spinning a basketball, on her shoulder; the sun, on her lower back; a Chinese symbol, on her right ankle; and a Gecko lizard, on her navel. She also has a navel ring.

Sketch of Unknown Male #1
Sketch of Unknown Male #2

The FBI is seeking information regarding the identity of these two individuals. They may have information about the disappearance of Amy Bradley in 1998. The first Unknown Male (#1) has been described as White; in his late 30's or early 40's; between 5'11" and 6'1"; balding; with red hair and a red beard. The second Unknown Male (#2) has been described as being in his early 30's; approximately 5'11"; with dark, wavy, shoulder length hair.

Individuals with information concerning this case should take no action themselves, but instead immediately contact the nearest FBI Office or local law enforcement agency. For any possible sighting outside the United States, contact the nearest United States Embassy or Consulate.
