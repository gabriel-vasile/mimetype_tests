Content Description

Abstract: Information depicted is a hydrographic survey of the Palm Beach Harbor Federal Channel, Palm Beach County, Florida. Hydrographic survey is performed to Hydrographic Survey Standards IAW (EM) 1110-2-1003. Survey runs from Station 0 00 of the Entrance Channel to Station 19 58 of the Turning Basin.
Purpose: none provided Hydrographic Surveys are required for Design; Construction; Operations and Maintenance of Civil Transportation & Water Resources Projects. Provide for Navigation of a Federal Channel.
Supplemental Information: This survey consists of 5 sheets.
