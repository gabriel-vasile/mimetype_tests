Interagency Program Inventory (July-August 2002)

 

Briefings on Climate Programs by CCSPO Agencies, July 2002

 

 

Research under the U.S. Climate Change Science Program is sponsored by 13 federal agencies:

  1. Agency for International Development
  2. Dept. of Agriculture
  3. Dept. of Commerce, National Oceanic & Atmospheric Admin. (also, National Institute of Standards and Technology)
  4. Dept. of Defense
  5. Dept. of Energy
  6. Dept. of Health and Human Services, National Institutes of Health
  7. Dept. of State
  8. Dept. of Transportation
  9. Dept. of the Interior, US Geological Survey
  10. Environmental Protection Agency 
  11. National Aeronautics & Space Administration
  12. National Science Foundation
  13. Smithsonian Institution

Detailed information is available on agency activities under the US Global Change Research Program.
