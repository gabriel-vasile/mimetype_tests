USS Cairo Gunboat 

Thirteen BIG Guns

 

 The Cairo carried thirteen big guns.  This 32 pounder Navy smoothbore, mounted on its original carriage, could fire a solid 32 pound cannon ball about a mile.  It required an eleven man crew to load, maneuver and fire. 
Armor  Capstan
