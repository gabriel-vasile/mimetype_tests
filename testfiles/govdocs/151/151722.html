Medical Encyclopedia: Glossitis

2008, Fact Sheet, Federal Publication, English
Web link: http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/ency/article/001053.htm

Description:

This publication describes what glossitis is, what the causes are, the incidence and the risk factors associated. It also provides information about its symptoms, signs and tests performed to confirm a diagnosis of glossitis, and treatment for glossitis. Additional information is provided about the prevention, prognosis of glossitis and when to call your doctor.

Availability information:

This publication is available only on the Internet.
