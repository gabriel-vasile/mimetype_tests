Business Conference on Corporate Governance - organized by "Kapital"

Presentation of Mr. Leonardo Peklar, Socius, Slovenia 		 

On March 12, 2008 in the hotel Aleksandar Palace in Skopje, the magazine Kapital organized a business conference on Corporate Governance. The conference was sponsored by USAID BEA.

The conference consisted of three panels, accordingly:

- Corporate Governance (CG) trends
- Transparency - The backbone of Corporate Governance
- Why and how much is Corporate Governance important for the investors
  Speakers at the event were experts from the Corporate Governance area, such as: Professor Goran Koevski, Samir Latif, Leonardo Peklar, Olli Vartanan etc.
