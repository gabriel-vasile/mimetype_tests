Returning to Your Home

EPA and MassDEP will continue to work closely with other state and local officials to ensure that Danvers residents have all the information they need to protect themselves from any potential environmental hazards.

If upon returning to your home, you observe household hazardous waste materials, such as paint thinners, pesticides or solvents, that have been tipped over and spilled, or any oil leaking around your home heating oil storage tank or structural damage to your basement that could have damaged a buried oil line, please contact John Fitzgerald of MassDEP at (978) 694-3308 or john.j.fitzgerald@state.ma.us
