Diabetic Retinopathy: What you should know
Pub No. EY-03

This booklet is for people with diabetic retinopathy and their families and friends. It provides information about diabetic retinopathy and answers questions about the cause and symptoms of this progressive eye disease. Diagnosis and types of treatment are described. A Spanish-language version is also available. (19 pages)
