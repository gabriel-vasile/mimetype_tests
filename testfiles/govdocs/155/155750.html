1. A device forming a base for a beehive comprising a framing having a shape that corresponds to the shape of the beehive, the framing having a hollow interior that includes, under a honeycomb disposed within the beehive and parallel to the honeycomb, at least one bar defining a pair of openings along opposite sides of the at least one bar and wherein the at least one bar is fixed to the framing by at least one crosspiece, wherein the at least one bar comprises several parallel bars, and wherein the spacing between the bars is between 1 and 5 millimeters.
