Silverleaf Whitefly

Scientific name: Bemisia argentifolii Bellows & Perring

Synonym: Bemisia tabaci Gennadius

Common names: Silverleaf whitefly, sweet potato whitefly

Native To: Possibly India

Date of U.S. Introduction: 1986

Images: Invasive.org and Google

Means of Introduction: Accidental

Impact: Damages crops by feeding on them and transmitting viruses

Current U.S. Distribution:

  • National Agricultural Pest Information System - Pest Tracker - Reported Status
