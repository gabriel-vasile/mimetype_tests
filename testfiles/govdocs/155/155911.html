Calendar Date Conversion Chart

  

To convert between the yearday format commonly used in satellite image names and year/month/day, simply strip off the four leading digits. These digits form the year. The remaining three digits are the day of the year, and using the table below you can determine the corresponding month and day of the month. Remember, use the first table for regular years, and the second table for leap years. For example, 1995100 would be April 10, 1995.
