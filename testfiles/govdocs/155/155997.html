LOCATION IDENTIFIERS FOR MEXICAN STATIONS

1. Purpose. Teletypewriter identifiers for U. S. and  Canadian stations are included in other publications, i.e.:

Canadian stations - Canadian Location Identifiers Published by the Meteorological Branch, Department of Transport, Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

U. S. Stations - Location Identifiers published by the Department of Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration.

There is no similar publication for Mexican stations. This chapter provides a listing of Mexican reporting stations and their identifiers.

2.    Listings. Mexican stations are listed alphabetically in 2.1. The identifiers are listed alphabetically in 2.2. Where two identifiers are shown, two reports are available. Reports from airport observing sites are listed in parentheses.
