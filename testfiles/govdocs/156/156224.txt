     This is a discrimination action under § 105(c) of the
Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977, 30 U.S.C. 
§ 801 et seq.

     After the hearing, a decision on liability was 
entered on December 5, 1994.

     A separate hearing on relief was held at El Paso, 
Texas, on May 9, 1995. On the second day of the hearing,
the parties reached a settlement agreement that was 
approved by the judge.

     Pursuant to the settlement agreement, the parties 
have moved to dismiss the case.

                              ORDER

     WHEREFORE IT IS ORDERED that the parties' motion to
dismiss is GRANTED and this proceeding is DISMISSED.
