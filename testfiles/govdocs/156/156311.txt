ENFORCEMENT PROCEEDINGS
  
  
IN THE MATTER OF JOHN ROONEY

     On  February 3, the Commission issued an Order, by consent, barring John
     Ryan Rooney, 36, from association with any broker or dealer with a right
     to  reapply for association after five years.  The Order follows a  Jan.
     16,  2004,  final  judgment  entered  against  Rooney  that  permanently
     enjoined him from future violations of Sections 10(b) and 14(e)  of  the
     Securities Exchange Act of 1934 and Rules 10b-5 and 14e-3 thereunder  in
     SEC  v.  Adrian  A.  Alexander, et al., Civil Action Number  00-CV-7290,
     USDC, SDNY.  (Rel. 34-49179; File No. 3-11391)
