List of Antimicrobial RED (Reregistration) Chemicals

Updated: 01/01/05

These are listings of EPA's Antimicrobial Reregistration Eligibility Decision (RED) documents for the various registered antimicrobial active ingredient chemicals. The lists are organized alphabetically by RED document titles, and by numerical order of their RED Case#. The dates indicate when the RED document was signed, completed, and released. Active ingredient chemical names included in the RED documents are also listed alphabetically together with their PC# (Pesticide Chemical Code Number). Only the chemical or commonly-used trade names are included in the lists. These lists were generated on June 10, 2003, and they will be updated periodically.
Basic information about certain active ingredient chemicals can also be obtained from the EPA IRIS (Integrated Risk Information Service) database and from the California Department of Pesticide Regulation (CDPR) Chemical Ingredient database using the PC#, active ingredient name, or the CAS# (Chemical Abstract Service number).

For further questions directly related to registration issues for these chemicals, please contact the PM (Product Manager) for that particular active ingredient chemical. For general information contact the Antimicrobial Division hotline at 703-308-0127, 703-308-6467 (FAX) or email at info_antimicrobial@epa.gov or hardy.michael@epa.gov or liem.david@epa.gov
