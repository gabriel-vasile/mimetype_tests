Agave Needle

With a little effort, it is possible to make a needle and thread. Cut an agave leaf off the plant, remove the hooks on the side of the leaf, but do not cut the spine off the tip. Carefully pound the leaf with a rock, scrape off the outer green layer, and twist the fibers into string. The needle can be used as is, or cotton or yucca cordage can be spliced onto the end. It is quite sharp, so be careful!

If you want an easier method for making string, raffia works well, and there are no spines. Click on the link for string-making directions. This is a Word document.
