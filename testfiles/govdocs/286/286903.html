Image of the Day

February 14, 2009
Bushfires in Southeast Australia

This false-color image from the Advanced Land Imager on NASA’s Earth Observing-1 satellite shows part of the 30,700-hectare (x-acre) Beechworth Fire—which killed at least two people according to ABC news reports—on February 10, 2009.
