KSC-96EC-0317 - STS-75 MS Maurizio Cheli of ESA arrives at SLF 


STS-75 Mission Specialist Maurizio Cheli of the European Space Agency
is greeted by wife Marianne after he and six fellow crew members
arrived at KSC's Shuttle Landing Facility. The second Shuttle flight
of 1996 will be highlighted by the re-flight of the Italian Tethered
Satellite System (TSS-1R). Liftoff is slated to occur during a
two-and-a-half window opening at 3:18 p.m. EST, Feb. 22.

 
                                               2/19/1996
