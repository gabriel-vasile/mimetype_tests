FANS (and related) Publications

If you have written a paper that reports measurements using FANS and you do not see it in the list below, or if the paper is listed but the information is incorrect or out of date, please inform Craig Brown.

If you write a paper that reports measurements using the DCS, please be sure to include an acknowledgement to NIST if there are no NIST coauthors. If you used the DAVE software package to treat your data please include an appropriate reference or acknowledgement.

Please be aware that all papers with NIST coauthors must be reviewed and approved by the Washington Editorial Review Board (WERB). Members of WERB are concerned not only with scientific merit but with three specific matters: measurement uncertainties, the use of SI units, and brand names. For more detailed information please click here and/or speak with your NIST coauthor(s).
