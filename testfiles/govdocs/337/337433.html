AREA FORECAST DISCUSSION
NATIONAL WEATHER SERVICE GOODLAND KS
441 PM MST MON FEB 9 2009


.PREV DISCUSSION... /ISSUED 227 PM MST MON FEB 9 2009/

.SYNOPSIS...
226 PM MST MON FEB 9 2009

ANALYSIS OF WATER VAPOR IMAGERY INDICATED INDICATED A DEEP
MID/UPPER LONGWAVE TROUGH SITUATED OVER THE WESTERN HALF OF THE
CONUS. STRONG NEGATIVELY-TILTED TROUGH WHICH BROUGHT BENEFICIAL
MOISTURE TO THE REGION OVERNIGHT CONTINUING TO TREK NORTHEASTWARD
FROM THE CENTRAL HIGH PLAINS INTO THE UPPER MIDWEST. NEXT EMBEDDED
SHORTWAVE TROUGH TO IMPACT THE CWA IS NOTED MOVING FROM SOUTHERN
NEVADA INTO UTAH.

FOLTZ
&&

.DISCUSSION...
226 PM MST MON FEB 9 2009

FORECAST CONCERNS WILL REVOLVE AROUND PROGRESSIVE FLOW PATTERN
WHICH WILL BRING A SERIES OF DISTURBANCES THROUGH THE CENTRAL HIGH
PLAINS DURING THE NEXT WEEK.

WEAK SHORTWAVE RIDGING CURRENTLY BUILDING INTO THE AREA IN THE
WAKE OF THE DEPARTING SHORTWAVE TROUGH. BREEZY SOUTHWEST WINDS AND
CLEARING SKIES HAVE ALLOW TEMPS TODAY TO REBOUND INTO THE 50S.
WILL SEE A SIMILAR RESULT FOR THE DAY ON TUESDAY THOUGH TEMPS WILL
BE TEMPERED TO SOME EXTENT BY INCREASING CLOUD COVER DURING THE
DAY AHEAD OF THE NEXT SHORTWAVE TROUGH SET TO PASS TUESDAY NIGHT
INTO WEDNESDAY. CURRENT DETERMINISTIC GUIDANCE CONTINUES TO
INDICATE BULK OF FORCING FOR ASCENT WILL REMAIN OVER SOUTHWEST
KANSAS AND NORTHERN OKLAHOMA BEFORE SLIDING EAST DURING THE DAY ON
WEDNESDAY. EC IS SLIGHTLY FURTHER NORTH AND STRONGER WITH THE H5
LOW SO MAINTAINED LOW SLIGHT CHANCE POPS OVER THE FAR SOUTHERN
PORTION OF THE CWA TO COVER POTENTIAL NORTHWARD JOG IN THE TRACK.

FAIRLY FLAT MID/UPPER RIDGING WILL BUILD INTO THE CWA ON THURSDAY
AS THE NEXT SHORTWAVE TROUGH COMES ONSHORE OVER CENTRAL
CALIFORNIA. NEXT SYSTEM BEARS WATCHING AS CURRENT PATH WOULD TAKE
IT JUST SOUTH OF THE CWA PUTTING US IN A FAVORABLE POSITION FOR
PRECIP. SLIGHT TEMPORAL AND SPATIAL DIFFERENCES BETWEEN THE EC AND
THE OPERATIONAL GFS BUT ENOUGH CONFIDENCE TO BOOST POPS INTO LOW
CHANCE RANGE. BUFR POINT SOUNDINGS WOULD INDICATE SNOW SHOULD BE
THE PRIMARY PRECIP PHASE WITH THE ENTIRE COLUMN BELOW 0 C. WILL
KEEP LOW SLIGHT CHANCE POPS GOING THROUGH 12Z SATURDAY BEFORE
BRINGING PRECIP CHANCES TO A CLOSE.

WILL SEE AFTERNOON TEMPS REMAINING SLIGHTLY BELOW CLIMATOLOGY
THROUGH THE END OF THE FORECAST PERIOD WITH ANOTHER SHORTWAVE
TROUGH DEPICTED TO IMPACT THE REGION SUNDAY INTO MONDAY. CARRYING
LOW SLIGHT CHANCE POPS SUNDAY INTO MONDAY WITH SNOW AGAIN THE
PRIMARY PHASE.

FOLTZ
&&

.AVIATION...
440 PM MST MON FEB 9 2009

FOR THE 00Z TAF CYCLE...VFR CONDITIONS EXPECTED THROUGH TAF CYCLE.
AT KMCK...SOMEWHAT GUSTY WINDS CURRENTLY IN AREA WILL BEGIN TO
SUBSIDE DURING THE NEXT FEW HOURS. GENERALLY SPEAKING FOR BOTH SITES,
WINDS WILL BE LIGHT FROM THE SOUTH AND SOUTHWEST AROUND 10KTS THIS
EVENING AND THEN SHIFT NORTHWEST OVERNIGHT/EARLY MORNING AND NORTH
BY THE END OF THE TAF CYCLE. SKIES WILL BE MOSTLY CLEAR EARLY IN
THE TAF PERIOD, BUT MORE MID-LEVEL CLOUDS LOOK TO APPROACH AREA BY
END OF CYCLE AS SYSTEM PASSES TO THE SOUTH.
