Norton Ad Blocker Issue with SAE
There is a known issue with SAE and Norton. Norton reads the SAE banner as an ad and the Ad Blocker then blocks the whole site. Disabling the Ad Blocker should allow you to access this site. Following are the basic instructions:
  • on your PC go to the Start button and click on All Programs
  • go to Symantec Client Security and click on Symantec Client Firewall
  • under Status & Settings: click on "Ad Blocking" and "Turn Off"
