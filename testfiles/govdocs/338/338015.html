Blocker’s Battery Arkansas Artillery

 

This unit was attached to Brig. Gen. James F. Fagan’s 2nd Brigade of Maj. Gen. Sterling Price’s Division, Lt. Gen. Theophilus H. Holmes’ District of Arkansas in Lt. Gen. Edmund Kirby Smith’s Trans-Mississippi Department and was commanded by Capt. W.D. Blocker. [Refer to Edwin Bearss’ The Vicksburg Campaign, Volume III, page 1244.]
