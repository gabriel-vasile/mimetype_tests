A CD-ROM game about leukemia for children.

Cost: USD 29.95 for families with children with cancer; USD 39.95 for all others;

To Order This Product, Contact:

Contact company for discounted/bulk rates at info@kidzwithleukemia.com

Product Type

CD-ROM (PC compatible)

Who Is This Product For?

Pediatric patients (ages 4 to 11) diagnosed with leukemia (ALL or AML), their families, friends, and support network; Healthcare providers as teaching tools and/or while explaining diagnosis/treatment to children with cancer and their families; Pediatric oncology faculty while teaching fellows and medical students; Libraries who loan them to patrons interested in learning more about the illness; Cancer camps, where camp counselors and the children with cancer can play with the CDs and learn more about their illness, share their thoughts and experiences; Survivor clinics, one of whom is handing this CD out to those who had childhood leukemia; Schools for instructing classmates of a child with cancer.

Where Is This Product Supposed to Be Used?

Inpatient/outpatient settings (including children¿s hospitals, pediatric oncology clinic waiting rooms and teaching areas); patient homes; elementary schools; libraries; childhood cancer camps, childhood cancer organizations and foundations.
