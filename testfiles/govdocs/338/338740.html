Cert Description
CERT "basic training" is offered in eight weekly sessions. The course is offered to anyone who lives or works in Vernonia. We provide all training materials plus basic safety gear. Follow-on training is being planned for related topics for our graduates throughout the year. Members are also encouraged to attend any of the basic training sessions as refresher. To keep members involved and motivated, we also look for ways to use CERT in community events.
