Profile: Cells single, in spray-like clusters or in small chains (the result of rapid vegetative cell division), but never occur in long filamentous/ribbon-like chains. Lack of a filamentous growth habit distinguishes Synedra from Fragilaria. University of Michigan, California Academy of Sciences, Luquillo Experimental Forest
