EPA Recognizes Energy Savings for Best Commuter Workplaces

Release date: 10/18/2005

Contact Information:

Contact: John Millett, 202-564-4355 / millett.john@epa.gov


(Washington, D.C. - October 18, 2005) EPA Administrator Stephen L. Johnson will brief reporters on release of list of Best Workplaces for Commuters from Fortune 500 Companies in a call-in telephone conference this afternoon. Nearly 90 companies from throughout the country made the list which is embargoed until midnight Eastern Time.

WHAT: Briefing on companies that made the list of Best Workplaces for Commuters from Fortune 500 companies.

WHO: EPA Administrator Stephen L. Johnson

WHEN: 3:45 p.m. Eastern Time
Tuesday, Oct. 18, 2005

Call starts at 3:45 p.m. sharp. Callers should start calling at 3:30 p.m. to ensure getting on the line.
Call-in number is 800-811-6040. Conference ID: 1605095
