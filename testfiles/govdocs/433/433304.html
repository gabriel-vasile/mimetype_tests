    Title MOLYBDENUM AND MOLYBDENUM-BASE ALLOYS. PART 1. HIGH-TEMPERATURE PROPERTIES OF A MOLYBDENUM-3 PER CENT THROIUM ALLOY. PART 2. EVALUATION OF A COLUMBIUM-ZIRCONIUM-TITANIUM CLADDING ALLOY
    Creator/Author Rengstorff, G.W.P. ; Root, G.S. ; Holden, F.C. ; Jaffee, R.I.
    Publication Date1960 Sep 01
    OSTI IdentifierOSTI ID: 4146593
    Report Number(s)NP-9214
    DOE Contract NumberNONR-1512(00)
    Resource TypeTechnical Report
    Resource RelationOther Information: Orig. Receipt Date: 31-DEC-61
    Research OrgBattelle Memorial Inst., Columbus, Ohio
    Sponsoring OrgUSDOE
    SubjectMETALS, CERAMICS, AND OTHER MATERIALS; ALLOYS; BEHAVIOR; CANNING; ENRICHMENT; HIGH TEMPERATURE; INTERMETALLIC COMPOUNDS; MECHANICAL PROPERTIES; MOLYBDENUM; MOLYBDENUM HYDROXIDES; NIOBIUM ALLOYS; OXIDATION; THORIUM; THORIUM ALLOYS; TITANIUM ALLOYS; ZIRCONIUM ALLOYS
    Description/Abstract In the first section of this report, a study was made of the high- temperature properties of a Mo-3% Th alloy to determine whether this alloy should be rejected as valueless or a full research program should be proposed. Results of this investigation show that thorium should be studied more intensively as an alloying addition for molybdenum. In the second section, an evaluation is presented of the oxidation behavior, mechanical properties, and cladding properties of Nb - 55 at.% Zr - 5 at.% Ti alloy. (W.L.H.)
