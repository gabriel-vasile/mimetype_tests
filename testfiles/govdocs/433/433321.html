Press Release
 
Emerson Meeting with Forest Service
to Discuss Opportunities

WASHINGTON  -  U.S. Representative Jo Ann Emerson (MO-08) and Mark Twain National Forest Supervisor Ronnie Raum will meet Monday, July 2, at 2:30 p.m. CDT to discuss timber sales and small-diameter timber projects.

“Small-diameter timber is often classified as a valueless product, but we have immense and important new opportunities to use this resource as a feedstock for biofuels.  I am glad the Forest Service shares my commitment to finding sources for alternative fuels.  We have a very valuable resource in Southern Missouri in the Mark Twain National Forest, and its sustainable management is an important economic driver for our economy.  I appreciate the job Mr. Raum is doing, and I look forward to meeting with him next week,” said Emerson.

WHO:           U.S. Representative Jo Ann Emerson
WHAT:         Meeting with U.S. Forest Service Supervisor for the Mark Twain N.F.
                     Ronnie Raum
WHEN:        Monday, July 2, from 2:30 to 3:30 p.m. CDT
WHERE:     Mark Twain National Forest Office, 405 Fairgrounds Road, Rolla
