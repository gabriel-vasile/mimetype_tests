NWS HYDROMETEOROLOGICAL PREDICTION CENTER CAMP SPRINGS MD
543 AM EDT WED MAR 11 2009


FINAL DAY 1...DAY 2 AND DAY 3 QPF DISCUSSION
VALID MAR 11/1200 UTC THRU MAR 14/1200 UTC
REFERENCE AWIPS GRAPHICS UNDER...PRECIP ACCUM - 24HR


THE 06Z MDL RUNS WERE IN VRY GOOD AGREEMENT WITH THE 00Z RUNS..SO
MADE ONLY VRY MINOR CHGS
TO THE PRELIM DAY 1 QPF.

DAY 1...

...SWRN TX ENEWD INTO THE ARKLATEX/SERN OK REGION...
SIGNIFICANT RNFL IS XPCD FM SWRN TX ENEWD INTO THE ARKLATEX/SERN
OK REGION THIS PD..WITH MDL QPFS IN PRETTY GOOD AGREEMENT ON THIS
SCENARIO.  IN THE WAKE OF THE STG SYS LIFTING NEWD INTO ERN
CANADA...THE MID TO UPR LVL FLO WL RMN OUT OF THE S-SW FM NRN
MEXICO INTO THE SRN PLAINS/LWR MS VLY..IN WHAT IS A WELL DEFINED
SRN BRANCH/SUBTROP STREAM ON RECENT SATL IMAGERY.  S/WV ENERGY
WITHIN THIS SRN STREAM AND A SLY LLJ OFF THE WRN GULF WL OVRRUN
THE LOW LVL COLD AIR SINKING SWD INTO THE REGION ERY IN THE
PD..SETTING THE STAGE FOR AN EXPANDING AREA OF OVRRNG PCPN ACRS A
LARGE PTN OF TX INTO THE ARKLATEX AND SERN OK.  HVIEST RNFL AXIS
LIKELY JUST N OF H85 BNDRY STRETCHING FM THE BIG BEND ENEWD TWDS
CNTRL AR..WITH SUSTAINED 30KTS OF LLJ..PWS ABV 1 INCH AND TRAINING
OF RNFL ECHOES SUPPORTING AT LEAST SOME 2-3 INCH AMTS WITHIN THIS
HVIEST RNFL AXIS.  AS STATED ABV..THE NAM AND GFS QPFS WERE GENLY
SIMILAR..ALTHO DID BEEF THE NAM QPF UP A BIT VCNTY OF SWRN TX MORE
LIKE THE GFS SOLN.  SOME WINTRY PCPN LIKELY ON THE NRN/NWRN FRINGE
OF THE PCPN SHIELD LT IN THE PD..OVR PTNS OF OK AND NRN AR..PLS
SEE THE QPFHSD FOR DETAILS ON THE WINTRY PCPN POTNL.


...NEW ENG...
WITH THE H5 SYS FCST TO LIFT RATHER QUICKLY ENEWD THRU SERN CANADA
THIS PD..ASSOCTD FNTL SYS WL ALSO SWEEP RAPIDLY THRU NEW ENG DURG
THE DAY WED..TRIGGERING MSTLY LGT TO LCLY MDT PCPN..WITH MAINLY
RAINS OVR SRN NEW ENG BUT WITH SOME ACCUMULATING SNOWS CNTRL INTO
NRN ME.  PWS AOA 1 INCH WL BE PULLED INTO THE REGION AHD OF THE
CDFNT..WHICH MAY SUPPORT SOME BRIEF HVIER PCPN RATES..BUT WITH THE
SYS BEING SO PROGRESSIVE..FELT THE GFS WAS A LTL TOO HVY WITH ITS
QPF OVR SRN NH AND ERN ME..AND LEANED TWDS SLGTLY LESSER QPF SOLNS
AS PER THE NAM..CANADIAN GEM AND 12Z ECMWF HIRES MDLS.


DAYS 2 AND 3...


...SOUTHERN PLAINS TO THE SOUTHEAST AND SOUTHERN MID ATLANTIC
STATES...
COLD FRONT TRAILING THE STRONG CYCLONE CURRENTLY SPINNING ACROSS
THE GREAT LAKES WILL LINGER FROM THE SOUTHEAST COAST BACK ALONG
THE GULF COAST...SETTING THE STAGE FOR A PROLONGED OVERRUNNING
EVENT FROM THE SRN PLAINS TO THE SOUTHEAST AND SRN MID ATL COASTS
AS SERIES OF SRN STREAM IMPULSES INTERACTS WITH MOISTURE FROM THE
GULF.  MODELS ARE SHOWING A FAIR AMT OF SPREAD IN THE HANDLING OF
THESE INDIVIDUALS IMPULSES AND GIVEN THEIR WEAK NATURE DO NOT HAVE
ANY HIGH CONFIDENCE IN THE FINER DETAILS OF ANY PARTICULAR MODEL
QPF AT THIS POINT.  THE NAM HOWEVER IS STRONGER AND FURTHER TO THE
N WITH A WAVE DEVELOPING OVER THE SRN MID ATL ON FRI THAN THE
MODEL CONSENSUS AND THEREFORE DID NOT DRAW PCPN AS FAR N AS THE
NAM IS DEPICTING.  FURTHER W BACK ALONG THE CTRL GULF COAST...THE
GFS IS ON THE STRONG SIDE AND NRN EDGE OF THE GUIDANCE WITH A WAVE
DEVELOPING THERE AND IN TURN BELIEVE AT THIS POINT ITS AMTS ACROSS
LA/MS ON DAY 3 ARE OVERDONE.  OTHERWISE...WITH LITTLE REASON SEEN
TO DISCOUNT ONE MODEL OVER THE OTHER...AN ENSEMBLE APPROACH WAS
TAKEN.  THE HEAVIEST AMTS OVER THE TWO-DAY PERIOD ARE EXPECTED TO
EXTEND FROM ERN TX TO THE CTRL GULF COAST STATES WHERE PWS ARE
EXPECTED TO REMAIN BETWEEN 1-1.5 INCHES THRU MUCH OF THE PERIOD
AND WHERE AN UPR JET COUPLET WILL OFFER AREAS OF ENHANCED UPR
DIVERGENCE.  AREAL AVGS OF 1-3 ARE NOT OUT THE QUESTION ACROSS
PORTIONS OF THE THIS REGION.


...SOUTHERN ROCKIES...
A DIFFUSE UPR TROF IS FORECAST TO SPREAD ACROSS THE
REGION...ENCOURAGING LGT TO OROGRAPHICALLY FOCUSED MDT AMTS ACROSS
THE REGION.  AS WITH THE DOWNSTREAM FEATURES...DO NOT HAVE ANY
HIGH CONFIDENCE IN ANY PARTICULAR MODEL`S HANDLING OF THE FINER
DETAILS HERE AND THEREFORE FOLLOWED A GFS/ECMWF/CMC GLOBAL BLEND.


...PACIFIC NORTHWEST...
PRECIPITATION IS EXPECTED TO RETURN TO WRN WA/OR ON DAY 3 AS AN
UPR RIDGE GIVES WAY TO A WELL-DEFINED TROF MOVING INTO WRN CAN AND
THE NWRN U.S.  A FASTER TREND IN THE ECMWF HAS PUT ITS TIMING INTO
BETTER AGREEMENT WITH THE GFS...HOWEVER THE GFS IS A BIT MORE
AMPLIFIED THAN THE REMAINING GUIDANCE AND THEREFORE ITS AMTS MAY
BE TOO MUCH ON THE HIGH SIDE AS WELL.
