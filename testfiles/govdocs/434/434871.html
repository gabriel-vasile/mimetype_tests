Dependable Computer Systems . . .
The trustworthiness of a computing system which allows reliance to be justifiably placed on the service it delivers. (IFIP WG10.4 definition) Reliability, Availability, Safety, Security.

Products, Tools, Services to enable the creation and maintenance of highly dependable computer systems and networked applications.

  • Protection against accidental and deliberate HW, SW, System, Network faults
  • Graceful degradation to a known state when faults are present
  • Simplified location and repair of faults
  • Testing and Evaluation of Systems and Networks
