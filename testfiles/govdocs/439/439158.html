Life of a Peace Corps Volunteer – info session

Plymouth State College

9/16/2008

Considering Peace Corps? Want a little more information about real life as a Volunteer? It will be exhilarating. It will be tough. It may be the adventure of your life. Find out about the texture of Peace Corps life.

At this informational meeting you will learn more about how you can spend two+ years living in another country, and what it may be like. Through your Peace Corps experience you will learn a new language (or a few), experience new cultures, ways of life, and gain a new perspective on the world. All while getting great international professional experience.

Peace Corps General Information Meeting
Tuesday, September 16, 2008
4:00pm - 6:00pm
Plymouth State - HUB 109

Peace Corps volunteers receive round trip transportation overseas; a monthly living allowance; full medical/dental coverage; possible student loan deferment, 24 vacation days per year and a $6075 readjustment allowance after completion of service.

Are you thinking of applying to the Peace Corps? Do it now! Peace Corps is VERY competitive this year. Find out why, and tips on applying.

The application process takes 6-12 months, and we always take applications on a rolling basis, but space is limited. Apply online at www.peacecorps.gov/application.
