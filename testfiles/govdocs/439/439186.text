                       HAWKEYE/EXPLORER 52 FORMATS


DATA FORMATS:

             Fix length logical records of 1296 bytes consisting
             of 32 bit Integer*4 and 64 bit Real*8 words in VAX
             integer and floating point format.

             Integer*4 format:

             Integer values are stored in twos complement
             representation with least significant byte first.

             bits   31   30 - - - - - - - - - - 0
                   | S | |   Binary number       |

                    S = 0(+), 1(-)

             Floating point format:  "Real*8"

             bits   15 14 - - - -  7 6 - - - - - - 0   Byte #
                     S |  Exponent | | Fraction    |   :A
             bits   31  - - - - - - - - - - - - - 16
                    |         Fraction             |   :A+2
             bits   47  - - - - - - - - - - - - - 32
                    |         Fraction             |   :A+4
             bits   63  - - - - - - - - - - - - - 48
                    |         Fraction             |   :A+6

             S = sign bit  0(+), 1(-)

       Spacecraft A/D conversion to D/A conversion used for all data
       is:  V = Raw * 0.00196 + 0.005.

          All temperatures are determined by monitoring the voltage
       across a parallel 15k precision resistor and thermistor. This
       combination is in series with a 22.1k ohm resistor and is
       connected to the 12.0 VDC bus. The resistance of the thermistor
       is given by:   R = 331500 * V / ( 180 - 37.1 * V ).
       The temperature in degrees C is given by:
          T = 390.4016 / ( 6.2988 - SQRT ( 32.895 - LN (R) ) - 273.
       Where SQRT = square root and  LN = logarithm base e.

       Conversions:      Battery Voltage, +28 volts and all solar
                         panel voltages are multiplied by 7.948.

                       +12 voltage bus voltage is multiplied by 3.01.

                         +12 volt bus current and solar panel currents
                         are given by I = V * 0.4 - 0.000175 * ( T - 22)

                         Battery current is given by
                         I = ( V - 0.29675 ) * 0.4
                             - 0.0005184 * ( T - 20.1 )

                         The electric antenna tip-to tip length in meters
                         is given by:
                            L = -9.72729 + 17.07355 * V - 1.17740 * V * V
                                 for V < 1.397
                              = -8.85695 + 16.18192 * V - 0.98509 * V * V
                                 for V > 1.397
