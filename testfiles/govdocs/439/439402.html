U.S. Cl. 349—114  [349/106] 9 Claims
 
1. A liquid crystal display device, comprising:
a liquid crystal display device panel including a liquid crystal layer and a pair of substrates sandwiching the liquid crystal layer, and a plurality of sub-pixels corresponding to different colors, a reflective region where reflective display is performed, and a transmissive region where transmissive display is performed, at least one sub-pixel having a transmissive region that is different sized from a transmissive region of another sub-pixel;
a reflector in each sub-pixel, each reflector being formed with a single aperture that corresponds to the transmissive region of the corresponding sub-pixel; and
color filters provided for the sub-pixels.
