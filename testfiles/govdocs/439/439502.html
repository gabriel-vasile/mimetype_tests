Marrowbone Dam Photo Gallery

NRCS is using a new construction technique to rehabilitate Marrowbone Creek Dam.  Rehabilitating the dam will ensure that it meets current dam design and safety criteria.  If left uncorrected, the lives of approximately 160 residents and 72 commuters would be at risk.  In addition, 39 homes, two businesses, a water treatment facility building, and four roads could be flooded, causing approximately $2,000,000 in damages.

To follow the dam rehabilitation progress, photos will be updated regularly until the project is complete. 

To View/Download a Photo:
  • Click on the photo to view it; the image will open in a new window
  • To download the image, right click it and choose "Save Picture As..."
