PRECIPITATION WILL AFFECT SOUTHEASTERN IDAHO SUNDAY WITH SNOW IN

ACROSS NORTHERN MOUNTAINS WITH LOCALLY HEAVIER AMOUNTS POSSIBLE. WINDS
WILL ALSO INCREASE CAUSING SOME BLOWING AND DRIFTING SNOW.
