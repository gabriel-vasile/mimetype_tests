Contact Us

Contacting a Patient

If you come to visit a patient, please see our maps for directions. You may also wish to review our visiting hours and policies page.

If you would like to send a card, gift or flowers to a patient, the mailing address is:

Patient Name (Patient Room Number)
c/o VA Medical Center
1500 Weiss Street
Saginaw, MI  48602

For the location of an inpatient or their phone number, dial the Medical Center at 989-497-2500 (or 1-800-406-5143).

Telephone Care

Please contact our telephone care line if you need medical advice, have a question about your medication, or need to schedule a non-urgent appointment. Your location determines which number to use.

  • During administrative hours (8am-4:30pm) call 989-497-2500 extension 11230 or 1-888-838-6446 extension 11230 
  • During non-administrative hours: 
    Toll-free 1-888-838-6446 

Schedule an Appointment

To schedule an appointment, please contact us at any of the numbers listed on our appointments page, or visit My HealtheVet.

Patient Representative

If you have a compliment, complaint, or other issue which you believe requires resolution, please contact one of our patient representatives. Their contact information is available on our customer service page.

Compensation and Pension

To speak with someone in compensation and pension, call the Medical Center at 989-497-2500 extension 11341 (or 1-800-406-5143 extension 11341).

Release of Information

If you need to get information from your medical records, please contact our Release of Information Office.

Veteran Fee Basis Contacts (for non-VA care and billing questions)

If you have questions regarding billing for non-VA services, please contact the Fee Basis Coordinator at 1-800-406-5143, extension 13155.

If you have questions regarding the transfer of a veteran from a non-VA facility to the VA, please contact the Transfer Coordinator at 1-800-406-5143, extension 13108.

If you have questions for the Clinical Utilization Review Nurse regarding admissions or discharges from a non-VA facility, please contact 1-800-406-5143, extension 11501.

Inquiry Routing and Information System

VA has establised an inquiry routing and information system which provides answers to frequently-asked questions. You may also use this system to submit a comment, question, or concern.

News Media

Members of the news media should contact our Public Affairs Office at (989) 497-2500 extension 13360.
