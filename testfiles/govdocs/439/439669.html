ICC-Customs-Connecting Wing - Washington, DC
Construction

As of July 27, 1998:

  • Phase 1 construction is proceeding on schedule and is 45% complete.
  • Phase 2 (ICC Building) construction is proceeding on schedule and is 16% complete.  The scope of work includes only building infrastructure (shell) and completion of one occupiable floor (7th), with design documents for the addition of the sixth floor and partial basement forthcoming.  Completion of remaining floors is predicated on the receipt of remaining funding.
  • Phase 3 (USCS & CW Buildings) construction is scheduled to commence upon receipt of anticipated FY99 funding.
